Rating plateaus happen for specific, identifiable reasons. Personal coaching finds them and fixes them systematically.

The Rating Improvement Training System

Phase 1: Diagnosis (Sessions 1–2)

Complete analysis of your current games to identify the top 3–5 reasons you're losing Elo. Each player's problems are unique — knowing exactly what to fix is the starting point of all improvement.

Phase 2: Tactical Foundation (Sessions 3–8)

Intensive tactical pattern training on the exact motifs you're missing. Fork, pin, skewer, discovered attack, and combination training calibrated to the tactical level just above your current ability.

Phase 3: Opening & Structure (Sessions 9–16)

Building a solid, personalized opening repertoire with White and Black. Understanding pawn structures that arise from your openings and the typical plans for each structure.

Phase 4: Endgame & Tournament Prep (Sessions 17+)

Core endgame technique — converting won positions, defending lost ones. Tournament-specific preparation including time management, psychological preparation, and match strategy.
