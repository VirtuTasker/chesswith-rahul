Why I Chose to Be a Personal Chess Coach

When I was learning chess, I noticed that students in group classes all progressed at different rates — but the instructor could only teach to the average. The fastest learners were bored; the slower ones were lost. I saw students giving up chess not because they lacked talent, but because they lacked personalized guidance. That's why I decided to offer exclusively one-on-one private chess coaching — because I believe every student deserves 100% of their coach's attention, every single session.
