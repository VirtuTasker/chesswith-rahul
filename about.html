But more importantly, competitive chess taught me how to teach. I realized that understanding a concept yourself is one thing - being able to explain it clearly to someone else is entirely different. This experience became invaluable when I started providing chess lessons and coaching.

üí° Why I Provide Chess Lessons at Home

I chose to provide private chess lessons and classes at students' homes rather than running a coaching center for several important reasons:

  • Personal Attention: In a student's home, I can give 100% focus to one student. No distractions, no divided attention.
  • Comfortable Environment: Students learn better in familiar surroundings where they feel relaxed and confident.
  • No Travel Hassle: Families save time and energy. I bring everything needed for chess coaching.
  • Parent Involvement: Parents can observe sessions, building trust and understanding of their child's progress.
  • Flexible Scheduling: I can accommodate different time preferences across Mumbai.

This personalized approach to chess training has proven incredibly effective. My students progress faster, enjoy learning more, and parents appreciate the convenience and transparency of home-based chess classes.
