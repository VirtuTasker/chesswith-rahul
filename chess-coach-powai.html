Yes — a significant portion of my Powai students come from the academic community near IIT Bombay. These children often have exceptional analytical aptitude and benefit from fast-tracked coaching that goes beyond basic chess into strategic depth quickly.

Is home chess coaching available on weekday mornings in Powai for homemakers? +

Yes — I have several Powai homemakers who book morning sessions (9 AM–12 PM) on weekdays. Hiranandani mornings are a great time for focused coaching when children are in school.
