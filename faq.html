Beginner Questions

Can complete beginners learn chess? ‚ñº
Absolutely YES! 90% of my students start with zero chess knowledge. I specialize in teaching beginners. My lessons explain everything from scratch in simple language. Within 4-6 weeks of weekly lessons, most beginners play complete games confidently. If you can learn anything new in life, you can learn chess!
Am I too old to learn chess? ‚ñº
You're NEVER too old! I've taught beginners aged 6 to 72. My oldest student started at 68 and now plays daily. Chess is mental, not physical - age doesn't matter. Whether you're 18, 35, 50, or 70+, if you want to learn chess, you absolutely can. I adapt teaching pace to your comfort level.
How long does it take to learn chess? ‚ñº
With weekly lessons: 4-6 weeks to play confidently, 2-3 months to play decent games. Learning chess is a journey - you'll keep improving forever! But beginners typically understand all rules and play complete games within 1-2 months of regular lessons.

Optional, light practice - nothing overwhelming! I suggest fun tactical puzzles (10-15 minutes) and playing 1-2 games between lessons. Completely optional. The practice helps, but there's no pressure. For kids, I make "homework" feel like playing games, not studying.
