Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 29, 2026

1. Introduction

ChessWithRahul ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our chess coaching services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, age (for students), location
  • Payment Information: Billing details for coaching sessions (processed securely)
  • Session Information: Chess game data, session recordings (if applicable), progress tracking
  • Communication: Messages exchanged via WhatsApp, email, or other channels

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide personalized chess coaching services
  • Schedule and conduct coaching sessions
  • Process payments for services
  • Communicate with you about your progress and sessions
  • Improve our teaching methods and services
  • Send important updates about our services

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in these cases:

  • With your explicit consent
  • With payment processors to handle transactions
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

When coaching minors (under 18), we obtain parental consent before collecting personal information. Parents have the right to review, update, or delete their child's information at any time.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Object to certain data processing activities

8. Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies to improve user experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

9. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services (like video conferencing platforms, payment processors) that have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review their policies.

10. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion at any time.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or through our website.
