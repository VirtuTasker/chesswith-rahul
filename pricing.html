Pricing FAQ

Is the first session really free? +
Yes — available to book now. No credit card, book via WhatsApp, confirm your slot. We send a trainer to your home, you experience a full 60-minute private session, and then decide if you want to continue. Sessions are available across all Mumbai areas.
How do I pay for a package? +
After your chess session, WhatsApp us to confirm your package. We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), NEFT/IMPS bank transfer, and cash. Payment is made upfront for the full package, which unlocks the discount and priority scheduling.
What if I'm not happy after a few sessions? +
If you're unhappy with your trainer, we'll arrange an alternative trainer at no charge. If you want to stop entirely, unused sessions can be refunded — contact us within 7 days. We want long-term happy students, not reluctant ones.
Can a package be shared between siblings? +
Yes. A 10 or 20 session package can be used by siblings in the same household — same trainer, same home. Just let us know when booking so we can plan sessions accordingly.
Are there separate prices for kids vs adults? +
No. Our pricing is the same for all students — kids, adults, beginners, and competitive players. The ₹1,200 per session (or package equivalent) applies across all programs and all areas of Mumbai.
