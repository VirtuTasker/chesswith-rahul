Complete Beginner Curriculum (6 Modules)

Systematic learning path from zero knowledge to confident play

1

Chess Basics & Piece Movement

Learn how to set up the board, understand coordinates (a1-h8), and master how each piece moves. We'll practice piece movement through simple exercises and mini-games until it becomes second nature.

Board Setup Piece Movement Coordinates Special Moves
2

Basic Chess Rules & Game Flow

Understand check, checkmate, and stalemate. Learn castling, en passant, and pawn promotion. We'll play complete practice games so you understand how a real game flows from start to finish.

Check & Checkmate Castling Stalemate Game Flow
3

Opening Principles & Development

Learn fundamental opening principles: control the center, develop pieces quickly, king safety. We'll build a simple opening repertoire with 2-3 solid openings that suit beginner play and teach good habits.

Center Control Development King Safety Simple Openings
4

Basic Tactics & Combinations

Master essential tactical patterns: forks, pins, skewers, and discovered attacks. Learn to spot these patterns in your games. We'll practice with hundreds of tactical puzzles to sharpen your pattern recognition.

Forks & Pins Skewers Double Attacks Tactical Puzzles
5

Basic Strategy & Planning

Understand fundamental strategic concepts: pawn structure, piece activity, weak squares, and space advantage. Learn to create simple plans in the middlegame and understand when to attack or defend.

Pawn Structure Piece Activity Planning Weak Squares
6

Essential Endgames & Game Analysis

Learn critical endgame fundamentals: king and pawn endings, basic checkmates (queen, rook), opposition. We'll analyze your games together to identify patterns in your mistakes and accelerate improvement through feedback.

Basic Mates King & Pawn Opposition Game Review

Your Learning Journey Timeline

What to expect as you progress from zero to confident player

Week 1-2

The Basics

Learn how pieces move, board setup, basic rules. Start playing simple practice games. You'll be able to play a legal game of chess!

Week 3-4

Opening Principles

Understand how to start games properly. Learn 2-3 simple openings. Start recognizing basic tactical patterns like forks and pins.

Month 2-3

Tactical Skills

Master fundamental tactics. Solve hundreds of puzzles. Develop board vision and pattern recognition. Win material consistently in games.

Month 4-6

Strategic Understanding

Learn to make plans, evaluate positions, understand pawn structures. Play with purpose and confidence. Ready for rated games or tournaments if interested!
