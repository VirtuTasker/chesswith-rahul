Your Beginner Chess Learning Journey

A clear, structured roadmap from absolute zero to confident chess player

1

Weeks 1–2: Foundation

Board setup, piece names, how each piece moves, chess notation basics, and the rules of the game. You'll play your first complete games by end of week 2.

2

Weeks 3–4: Basic Strategy

Opening principles (control the center, develop pieces, castle), basic checkmate patterns (back rank, ladder, smothered mate), and how to think a move ahead.

3

Weeks 5–8: Tactical Vision

Tactical patterns — forks, pins, skewers, discovered attacks. Simple endgames (King + Queen vs King, King + Rook vs King). You'll start winning games consistently.

4

Month 3+: Intermediate Growth

Personalized opening repertoire, positional understanding, pawn structures, and competitive game preparation. You'll be ready to play in local tournaments with confidence.
