About Henil Dharod

Henil Dharod is ChessWithRahul Academy's FIDE-rated trainer based in Chembur — covering Chembur East, Chembur West, and RCF Colony for home sessions, with online coaching available to students anywhere in India. His official FIDE ID is 564069389, giving students and parents a verifiable, internationally recognised rating to check his competitive credentials directly on the FIDE website.

As an active rated player, Henil brings genuine tournament experience into every lesson — not just textbook theory, but the calculation discipline and time-management habits that separate club-level play from competitive results. He is especially effective with students who want to move beyond casual chess into serious, rating-focused improvement.

Chembur has a strong base of students preparing for school and district-level chess events, and Henil structures his curriculum specifically around that goal — building opening repertoires, tactical pattern recognition, and endgame technique in a clear progression.
