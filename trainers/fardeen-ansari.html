About Fardeen Ansari

Fardeen Ansari is ChessWithRahul Academy's verified trainer for Goregaon West, offering both home visits and online coaching — making him one of only two trainers in the network who can serve students across India through online sessions. With 28+ students trained and 3+ years of coaching experience, Fardeen has built a strong reputation in Goregaon for his friendly, encouraging approach — particularly effective with younger children who are nervous about a new activity. He creates a safe and exciting learning environment where mistakes are treated as learning opportunities, not failures.

At ₹600 per session, Fardeen is ChessWithRahul Academy's most accessible trainer — making quality chess coaching available to families across all income levels in Goregaon. Fardeen is particularly strong at teaching beginners — both children and adults who have never touched a chess board. His patience in the first 5–6 sessions — when students are still building basic fluency — is exceptional, and his retention rate (how many students continue past month 1) is among the highest in the academy.
