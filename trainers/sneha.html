About Sneha Patil

Sneha Patil is ChessWithRahul Academy's central Mumbai trainer, covering Dadar, Bandra, Matunga, Mahim, Sion, and the surrounding heart-of-the-city neighbourhoods. Based in Dadar East, Sneha offers the fastest session availability for students across central Mumbai — an area that has historically been underserved by quality home chess coaching.

Sneha's coaching is calm, methodical, and exceptionally patient — qualities that make her particularly effective with adult beginners and with students who have had frustrating experiences with chess in the past. She specialises in rebuilding confidence alongside chess skill, working at the student's pace without any pressure to rush through material.

Her background in education — she holds a teaching qualification — gives Sneha a structured approach to curriculum design that sets her apart from many self-taught coaches. She documents each student's progress formally, sets measurable milestones, and shares monthly progress summaries with parents or the students themselves.

Central Mumbai families appreciate Sneha's reliability and communication — she is known for being consistently punctual, professionally prepared, and warm with students of all ages. Her waiting list for new students has grown substantially over the past year, so early booking is advisable.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Sneha cover Bandra West for home coaching? +
Yes — Bandra West is one of Sneha's active coaching zones. WhatsApp to confirm slot availability for your specific area within Bandra.
How does Sneha approach adult beginners who find chess intimidating? +
Sneha starts with the simplest possible game structure — just kings and pawns — and builds upward. She never rushes, never makes the student feel slow, and celebrates every milestone. Most adult beginners are playing real games with confidence within 3–4 sessions.
Can Sneha teach children and adults in the same family? +
Yes — several families in Dadar and Bandra have both a child and a parent training with Sneha, sometimes in back-to-back sessions. It's a great way to make chess a family activity.
What is Sneha's availability for weekday sessions in Dadar? +
Sneha has weekday morning, afternoon, and evening slots available. Weekend slots fill up faster. WhatsApp early to secure preferred timing.
