About Vikram Nair

Vikram Nair is ChessWithRahul Academy's eastern Mumbai specialist, covering Powai, Mulund, Chembur, and Thane. An engineer by training, Vikram brings a systematic, analytical approach to chess coaching that resonates particularly well with the tech-professional community concentrated in Powai's Hiranandani township and surrounding areas.

His coaching philosophy is built around structured thinking: Vikram teaches students to approach chess problems the same way an engineer approaches a complex system — break it down, identify the key variables, calculate the most efficient path. This approach builds deep chess understanding rather than just pattern memorisation.

Vikram is equally effective with children and adults. For younger students, he translates the analytical framework into age-appropriate activities and games. For adult professionals in Powai — many of whom are learning chess as a cognitive workout alongside demanding careers — he designs session-efficient curricula that maximise improvement per hour.

Powai and Mulund have historically had limited access to quality in-home chess coaching. Vikram fills this gap as the area's dedicated ChessWithRahul trainer — bringing champion-standard coaching directly to homes across eastern Mumbai.
