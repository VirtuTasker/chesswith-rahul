About Manthan Chandivade

Manthan Chandivade is one of ChessWithRahul Academy's most experienced and highest-rated trainers, holding the Gold Trainer badge for his consistent teaching quality and exceptional student outcomes. Based in Malad East, Manthan has trained 42+ students across Malad, Goregaon, and the surrounding Western Line suburbs.

Manthan's strength lies in building a strong chess foundation in children — he is patient, methodical, and has a natural ability to explain chess concepts in age-appropriate language that kids genuinely understand and retain. Parents in Malad consistently praise his ability to keep children engaged across multiple months of weekly coaching.

At ₹800 per session, Manthan provides exceptional value — his coaching quality matches coaches charging significantly more, and his track record of student improvement is among the strongest in the academy's trainer network. Manthan's home visit approach means he arrives at your Malad home with a professional chess board, clock, and a prepared lesson plan tailored to the student's exact level and previous session progress. He also sends puzzle homework via WhatsApp after each session.
