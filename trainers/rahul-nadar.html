Frequently Asked Questions

Does Rahul teach online as well as home sessions? +
Yes — Rahul offers both home visits across the Western Line and structured online sessions via Google Meet with shared interactive boards. Online sessions are equally structured and include post-session puzzle homework.
What is the 30-Day Chess Foundation method? +
Rahul's structured beginner curriculum: Week 1 covers pieces and basic tactics; Week 2 introduces openings and checkmate patterns; Week 3 builds positional thinking; Week 4 integrates everything into full practice games with analysis. Most beginners play their first real game in the very first session.
Can Rahul prepare my child for chess tournaments? +
Yes — tournament preparation including opening systems, time management, and match-day psychology is one of Rahul's core specialisations. Several of his students compete at school and district level.
How many sessions per week do you recommend for kids? +
Two sessions per week is optimal for school-age children — enough to build momentum, reinforce learning between sessions, and produce visible results within the first month.
