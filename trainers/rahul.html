About Rahul

Rahul is the founder of ChessWithRahul Academy and Mumbai's most recognised personal chess coach. A Multiple Inter-College Chess Champion with a 4.9★ Google rating and over 52 students trained, Rahul has built a reputation for delivering measurable, consistent improvement at every level — from complete beginners to competitive Elo climbers.

Kandivali East is where Rahul is based — making the entire Western Line his primary coaching territory. His proximity means zero travel fatigue for students in Kandivali, faster session availability, and a coach who genuinely knows the local community, the schools, and the learning culture of Mumbai's northern suburbs.

What sets Rahul apart is his philosophy: chess should be intellectually stimulating and emotionally fulfilling, not just a technical exercise. He blends opening theory with tactical puzzles, endgame technique with psychological game preparation — building well-rounded players who perform under pressure and think ahead with confidence.

Beyond his personal coaching, Rahul has trained and mentored all trainers in the ChessWithRahul Academy network — setting the quality standard that every student on the platform can expect. When you book with any trainer at ChessWithRahul, you're booking someone personally vetted by Rahul.
