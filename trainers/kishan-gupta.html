About Kishan Gupta

Kishan Gupta is ChessWithRahul Academy's verified home chess trainer for Borivali East — covering the northern Mumbai suburb and extending to Dahisar on select days. With 20+ students and a growing reputation, Kishan is one of the academy's most consistent performers.

Kishan's coaching is direct and results-focused — he sets specific, measurable milestones with every student in the first session and tracks progress toward those targets. Parents appreciate his honest communication: he tells you exactly where the student stands and what they'll achieve in the next 8 sessions.

Borivali East has many families whose children participate in inter-school and district chess competitions. Kishan knows the Mumbai junior chess circuit and builds tournament-specific preparation into student programmes months ahead of major events.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Kishan cover Dahisar? +
Yes — Kishan covers Dahisar on select days as an extension of his Borivali zone. WhatsApp to confirm availability for your specific Dahisar location.
Can Kishan prepare my child for a district chess competition? +
Yes — Kishan is familiar with Mumbai's junior chess circuit and builds tournament preparation into student programmes months ahead of major district-level competitions.
What is the best age to start coaching with Kishan? +
Kishan coaches from age 6 upward. The ideal starting age is 7–9 when children have enough focus for structured sessions but are still in the peak phase of pattern-learning.
How does Kishan measure progress between sessions? +
Kishan sets concrete milestones in session one and reviews them every 4 sessions. He also assigns and reviews puzzle homework weekly — progress is measurable and never vague.
