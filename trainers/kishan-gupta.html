About Kishan Gupta

Kishan Gupta is ChessWithRahul Academy's verified home chess trainer for Borivali East, covering the Borivali suburb comprehensively and extending into Dahisar and Kandivali East on select days. With 20+ students trained and growing, Kishan is one of the academy's most consistent and reliable coaches.

Kishan's coaching approach is direct and results-focused — he sets clear milestones for every student in the first session and works systematically toward those targets. Parents appreciate his honest communication: he tells you exactly where the student is, what they need to work on, and how long improvement will take.

Borivali East is home to many ambitious families whose children participate in inter-school and district-level chess competitions. Kishan's familiarity with the local chess circuit means he knows which tournaments are upcoming, what level of preparation is needed, and how to get a student from casual player to competition-ready efficiently.

At ₹700 per session, Kishan provides Borivali's most cost-effective quality home chess coaching. He is particularly effective with children aged 7–15 and with adults returning to chess after a break.
