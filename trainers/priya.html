About Priya Sharma

Priya Sharma is ChessWithRahul Academy's dedicated kids chess specialist, covering Andheri West, Jogeshwari, Versova, and Santacruz. With 4 years of coaching experience and 30+ students taught, Priya has developed a teaching methodology that makes chess genuinely exciting for young children — not just educational.

Her approach is built around one core belief: children learn best when they're playing, not listening. Every session Priya designs starts from a game or puzzle — and the theory emerges from what the child discovers, rather than being presented as dry information. This game-first, curiosity-led approach produces children who love chess and keep coming back.

Priya is particularly in demand among families in Andheri West's residential neighbourhoods — Lokhandwala, Versova, and 4 Bungalows — where she has built a strong reputation over four years of consistent, punctual, warm coaching. Many of her current students were referred by parents of her previous students.

All of Priya's students are personally reviewed by Rahul quarterly to ensure teaching quality meets the ChessWithRahul Academy standard. Priya's sessions include homework puzzles sent via WhatsApp after each lesson, and progress notes shared with parents monthly.

What Priya Teaches

🎮

Play-First Learning

Kids learn by doing — small games with increasing complexity before theory is introduced. Engagement first, structure second.

🧩

Tactical Puzzles

Age-appropriate daily puzzles targeting the patterns kids encounter most — simple captures, one-move checkmates, basic forks.

📚

Piece Stories

Priya uses character-based storytelling to teach piece movements and strategy — turning abstract rules into memorable narratives.

🏆

School Tournament Prep

For students targeting school-level competitions — specific opening preparation, time management, and match-day psychology.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the youngest age Priya teaches? +
Priya has successfully coached children as young as 5 years old. She uses piece-movement games and stories that make chess accessible and fun for very young learners.
Does Priya cover Andheri East as well? +
Yes, Priya covers both Andheri East and West, as well as Jogeshwari, Versova, and Santacruz. WhatsApp to confirm availability for your specific sub-area.
How does Priya keep young children engaged for a full 60 minutes? +
Priya structures sessions into 3–4 short activities rather than one long lesson. Games, puzzles, mini-tournaments, and story-based exercises keep kids engaged and energised throughout.
Does Priya share updates with parents after each session? +
Yes — Priya sends a brief WhatsApp message after every session summarising what was covered, what went well, and a homework puzzle for the week.
