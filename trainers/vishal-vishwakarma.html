About Vishal Vishwakarma

Vishal Vishwakarma is a verified ChessWithRahul Academy trainer based in Andheri East, covering one of Mumbai's most densely populated and educationally competitive areas. With 33+ students trained and 4+ years of coaching experience, Vishal has established himself as Andheri East's go-to home chess trainer.

Vishal's teaching style is calm, encouraging, and highly structured — qualities that parents of school-age children in Andheri particularly value. He works with students from complete beginners to intermediate competitive players, building a solid technical foundation before introducing advanced concepts. His coaching sessions are never rushed — every student leaves understanding what they covered and why.

Andheri East's professional community has found Vishal's coaching especially effective for their children, many of whom are enrolled in high-pressure CBSE and ICSE schools around MIDC and Marol. Chess under Vishal's guidance builds the focus, analytical thinking, and patience that directly supports academic performance.

At ₹700 per session, Vishal offers Andheri's most affordable quality home chess coaching. He covers Andheri East and West comprehensively, and also serves students in Marol, J.B. Nagar, Chakala, and nearby Jogeshwari East on select days.
