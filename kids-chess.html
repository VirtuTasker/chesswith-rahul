Kids Chess Coaching — Common Parent Questions

What age can my child start private chess lessons? +

Children as young as 6 years old can start learning chess through personal coaching. At this age, Rahul uses fun puzzles, stories about chess pieces, and mini-games to introduce concepts in an age-appropriate way. The earlier children start, the stronger foundation they build.

How is personal coaching better than school chess classes? +

School chess clubs typically have 15–30 students with one instructor — your child gets only 2–3 minutes of individual attention per session. Personal coaching means 60 full minutes focused entirely on YOUR child, their specific weaknesses, their pace, their goals. This is why students improve 3X faster with private coaching.

How quickly will my child improve with a personal chess coach? +

Most children can play complete, confident chess games within 6–8 weeks of personal coaching. Children who start playing tournaments often win their first medals within 3–6 months. The speed of improvement depends on how frequently sessions are scheduled and how much the child practices between sessions.
