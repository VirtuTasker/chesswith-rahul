Your Learning Journey

What to expect as an adult learner

Month 1

Foundation Building

Learn how pieces move, basic rules, and start playing complete games. Many adults master this in just 4-6 sessions because you can focus better than children.

Month 2-3

Tactical Understanding

Develop pattern recognition and tactical awareness. Learn common tactical motifs like forks, pins, and skewers. Start winning material consistently in games.

Month 4-6

Strategic Growth

Understand positional concepts, planning, and when to attack or defend. Play with purpose and confidence. Ready for rated online games or club play.

Month 6+

Continuous Improvement

Keep learning and enjoying chess. Join clubs, play tournaments (optional), or simply enjoy games with friends. Chess is a lifelong journey of discovery.
